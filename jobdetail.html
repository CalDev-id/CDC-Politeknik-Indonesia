Job Description

The 12 month Management Trainee Program enables graduates to gain real insights into life at Air Liquide. It offers a structured program that allows for an in-depth exploration of interest in the chemicals industry and develops their professional skills and competencies.

Requirements:

  • Bachelor Degree in Engineering (Chemical, Industrial, Metallurgical, Mechanical, Environmental, Electrical)
  • Minimum GPA 3.25
  • Excellent verbal and written English
  • Strong analytical thinking and presentation skills

Selection Process:

  • Shortlisted candidates will be invited to the company presentation and Focus Group Discussion (FGD) session on August 4th, 2022 at University of Indonesia (UI).
  • The transportation cost to the venue shall be borne by the candidate.
